## Status Values

- `draft`: not ready for downstream use.
- `ready`: approved and usable by the next stage.
- `partial`: usable only for stated scope.
- `blocked`: missing required decision/evidence.
- `obsolete`: replaced by a newer version.

## Version Naming

Prefer stable, readable names:

- `requirement-v1.0`
- `prototype-v1.0`
- `model-v1.0`
- `api-v1.0`
- `backend-v1.0`
- `frontend-v1.0`
- `test-v1.0`
- `release-v1.0`
